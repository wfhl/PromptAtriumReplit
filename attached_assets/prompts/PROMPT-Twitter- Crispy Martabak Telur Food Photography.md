### 📝 Core Summary
This prompt details a hyper-realistic macro food photograph of a crispy martabak telur, captured mid-action as it tears open with its hot filling bursting outward. It emphasizes dynamic elements like suspended flecks, visible steam, and a glossy oil sheen, alongside intricate texture details of the wrapper and interior. The scene is set with warm Indonesian street-stall lighting, including tungsten and charcoal warmth with a dramatic rim light. Specific camera settings such as a Nikon Z9 look, 105mm macro lens, shallow depth of field, fast shutter freeze, and bokeh of night market signage are requested to achieve an editorial-quality image.

### 🔄 Flows & Concepts
- Dynamic Food Photography: Capturing food in an active state (tearing, bursting) with elements suspended in motion.
- Sensory Detail: Emphasis on steam, glossy oil sheen, crispy texture, and hyper-real crumbs to enhance visual appeal.
- Authentic Atmosphere: Incorporating "Warm Indonesian street-stall lighting, tungsten + charcoal warmth" for a specific cultural ambiance.
- Technical Precision: Directing specific camera equipment (Nikon Z9, 105mm macro) and settings (shallow depth of field, fast shutter freeze, dramatic rim light) for a professional outcome.
- Hyper-realism: Focusing on "ultra high texture detail" to create an exaggeratedly realistic and appealing food image.
